## Abstract

This document provides a semi-analytic determination of the gravitational scale in the SPU framework. Newton's constant is shown to emerge dynamically from the collective stiffness of the SPU medium, without introducing a fundamental Planck scale or additional free parameters. The resulting estimate is consistent with the observed strength of gravity and establishes a quantitative bridge between gauge unification and emergent gravity.
